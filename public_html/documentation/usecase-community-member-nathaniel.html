Nathaniel Neighbor Use-Case

Use-case: Nathaniel wants to find someone that is able to fabricate a replacement part for a broken component from an old faucet.

Pre-condition: Nathaniel is browsing the ABQ Maker-41 website.

Post-condition: Nathaniel found someone local that is able to create a 3d model of his broken faucet piece and print it with their 3d printer. He has reached out through the website for further information from this person.

    Interaction Flow
  • Nathaniel spends some time scrolling through the landing page of the “Maker” site as he’s not sure what he’s looking for.
  • None of the links on the main page seem to fill his needs, then he notices a search bar.
  • He types “fabrication” into the search bar as he’s not sure what or who it is he’s actually looking for.
  • Many categories come up from his search. He notices one for welders and metalwork. Nathaniel follows the button click to the listing for metal workers.
  • None of these listings seem to fit what he’s looking for. So he go back to the search results and scrolls a little further down the list. He notices there’s a listing for “3d Printing”. He clicks on the 3d Printing button.
  • A page comes up with about 20 Makers offering 3d printing services. He starts reading the postings. He clicks on the first listing he notices has pictures of mechanical/technical appearing 3d printed items.
  • On the listings page, he notices a portfolio of this person’s past work. He also notices some pricing options and a review section. Nathaniel clicks on the review section.
  • He scrolls through the reviews and notices this “Maker” has mostly positive reviews. He then clicks on the portfolio and looks at a few pictures.
  • Nathaniel notices some pricing on the site but is not sure how this would apply to the faucet piece he needs to be fabricated. He notices a contact seller button and clicks on it.
  • A small box pops up with fields to leave his contact information and a messagegit. He leaves his email address, phone number, and types a brief message requesting more information.
  • Nathaniel hits the send button and exits the site. He eagerly awaits a reply from this well-reviewed local Maker!
