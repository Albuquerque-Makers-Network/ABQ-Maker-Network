Maria Sandoval Use-Case

Use-case: Maria recently heard of the ABQ Maker Network from a facebook group she is a part of. She wants to sign up for an account so that she can start connecting with folks who may be interested in commissioning a lawn ornament from her.

Pre-condition: Maria navigates to the ABQ Maker Network site from her phone.

Post-condition: Maria has signed up for an account and set up her maker profile.

    Interaction Flow
  • Maria opens the ABQ Maker Network site.
  • The site displays the home screen, with a preview of the maker profile grid.
  • Maria clicks on the "Creator Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the screen.
  • The site opens the Creator Sign-up Page.
  • Maria fills out the static form on the page. She enters her name, about me, contact information, and services provided in the form and then clicks "Sign Up".
  • The site redirects her to the creator account page, displays her "About me", "Portfolio Pieces", "Services Provided", and "Pricing." The pricing and portfolio pieces sections are empty.
  • Maria selects "Add/Remove Portfolio Elements" under the portfolio section.
  • The site highlights the portfolio section and displays a button titled "Upload Photo."
  • Maria clicks on the "Upload Photo" button.
  • The site shows a pop-up requesting that Maria select where the photo is located.
  • Maria selects "My Phone" and then selects several photos from her camera roll.
  • The site shows an "uploading" message and then her photos appear in the portfolio photos stack.
  • Maria clicks on "Edit" in the pricing section.
  • The site opens an editable form under "Pricing"
  • Maria enters her pricing information and clicks "save"
  • The site closes the form and Maria's updates are applied to the Pricing section.
  • Maria clicks "Submit" at the bottom of the account page.
  • The site exits edit mode and all of Maria's profile information is updated.
