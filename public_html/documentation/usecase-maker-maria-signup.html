Maria Sandoval Use-Case

Use-case: Taylor recently completed a substantial project that he wants to add to his portfolio to better showcase his abilities.

Pre-condition: Taylor is logged into his creator account and on the site home-page.

Post-condition: Taylor uploaded a photo of a 40-inch tall matchstick model of the Taj Mahal to his profile portfolio. He placed the new photo at the top of the portfolio and removed an old photo.

    Interaction Flow
  • Taylor selects the "Account Icon" in top-right corner of the screen.
  • The site brings up Taylor's account home page which displays his "About me", "Portfolio Pieces", "Services Provided", and "Pricing."
  • Taylor selects "Add/Remove Portfolio Elements"
  • The site highlights the portfolio section. Each photo listed shows a small "x" on it for possible removal. Below the photos is a button titled "Upload Photo."
  • Taylor clicks on the "Upload Photo" button.
  • The site shows a pop-up requesting that Taylor select where the photo is located.
  • Taylor selects "My Phone" and then selects the specific photo.
  • The site shows an "uploading" message and then the photo appears at the bottom of the portfolio photos stack.
  • Taylor selects and drags the photo above the other photos because he wants it prominently displayed.
  • The site shows the new photo at the top of the stack.
  • Taylor sees a photo he no longer wants displayed and selects the "x" to remove it.
  • The site removes the older photo from the portfolio photos.
  • Taylor selects "Submit."
  • The site exits "edit mode" and displays Taylor's account page normally.
